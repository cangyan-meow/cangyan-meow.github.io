随便学点 | 一个关于语言学习方法的视频
2024-01-04 15:39:57 # 随便学点 #猫のLanguage

找到了一个讲语言学习的视频，博主很厉害，掌握了多门不同语系的语言。这个视频主要讲英语母语者如何从零开始学外语，但中文母语者学英语、二外也可以参考，只不过大纲上的内容需要做相应调整。

How to acquire any language NOT learn it

该视频给出的学习技巧的重点是用 comprehensible input 替代死板的 word memorize 和 grammar study。与本人情况适配的大纲总结如下：

  • Comprehensible input
    • e.g. story listening
  • Don’t study grammar
    • You don’t have time to think about correctness before you say the sentence
    • Thinking about grammar first prevents you from fluency
    • Learn grammar after you can speak fluently
  • Find the right class
    • The natural approach: no grammar, no correction, no other language
    • TPRS: teaching proficiency through reading and storytelling
  • Find the right instructor
    • Things that are easy to understand
  • Decide the language and time (Depending)
    • Don’t spend your time on reading and writing, listen and speak!
  • Find language partners
    • Friends, coworkers, language consults; language exchange; APPs…
  • The magic
    • Magazines: picture, ask what’s this/ what is she doing/ why…
    • Children’s stories: retell the stories
  • TPR(Total physical response)
    • Ask your partner to give you a list of commands, verbs, or actions
  • Read to learn grammar smoothly
  • Input + 1
    • Input you already know + something ramping up
  • Repeat the recordings
